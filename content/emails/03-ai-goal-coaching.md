## How AI Goal Coaching Works

Think of it as a conversation with a really patient coach who asks the right questions.

Here's a real example:

**You**: "I want to be healthier"

**Entmoot AI**: "Great goal! Let's make it specific. What does 'healthier' look like for you? Exercise? Nutrition? Sleep? All of the above?"

**You**: "Mostly exercise. I want to work out more."

**AI**: "Perfect. How many times per week feels achievable? 3 times? 5 times?"

**You**: "Maybe 3 to start."

**AI**: "Excellent. What type of exercise? And how long - 20 minutes? 30? 45?"

**You**: "30-minute walks. I can do that."

**AI**: "Wonderful! Here's your SMART goal:

**'Walk for 30 minutes, 3 times per week'**

- ✅ Specific: 30-minute walks
- ✅ Measurable: 3 times per week
- ✅ Achievable: Based on your schedule
- ✅ Relevant: Improves health
- ✅ Time-bound: Weekly cadence

Should I break this into a weekly checklist for you?"

---

See what happened there?

"Be healthier" became "Walk 30 minutes, 3x/week."

One is a wish. The other is a plan.

## The SMART Framework

Our AI coaches you through these 5 criteria:

**Specific**: Exactly what you'll do (not "exercise" but "walk")
**Measurable**: How you'll track it (3 times per week)
**Achievable**: Realistic given your life (not 7 days/week if you've never exercised)
**Relevant**: Connects to what you care about (health, family, career)
**Time-bound**: When you'll do it (weekly, monthly, quarterly)

This isn't new science. It's proven goal-setting methodology.

The AI just makes it easy to apply.

## Educational Content

### Why SMART Goals Work (Optional sidebar)

Research shows SMART goals are 90% more likely to be achieved than vague intentions.

Why?

Your brain needs:
- **Clarity** (specific)
- **Feedback** (measurable)
- **Confidence** (achievable)
- **Motivation** (relevant)
- **Urgency** (time-bound)

SMART goals provide all five.
