## App Store Description

### Short Description (Promotional Text - 170 characters)

Plan your day, build lasting habits, and achieve your goals together. Entmoot helps families coordinate and celebrate success with AI-powered goal coaching.

### Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Plan Together. Grow Together.**

Entmoot is the family planning app that helps everyone stay aligned, build positive habits, and achieve meaningful goals. Whether you're coordinating daily schedules, tracking weekly reviews, or setting ambitious annual objectives, Entmoot provides the structure and motivation your family needs to thrive.

**Daily Planning Made Simple**

Start each day with intention. Set your focus, prioritize your top 3 tasks, and track your non-negotiable habits. The Today view gives everyone a clear picture of what matters most, helping your family move through each day with purpose and clarity.

- Set daily intentions to stay focused
- Manage your top 3 priorities
- Track habits with streak counters
- Add and complete daily tasks
- Evening reflection prompts for mindful closure

**Goal Setting with AI Coaching**

Transform vague wishes into achievable SMART goals. Our AI coach helps refine your goals to be Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. Whether it's a daily habit or a yearly ambition, Entmoot guides you from intention to completion.

- Create goals at any time scale: daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annual
- AI-powered goal refinement with actionable suggestions
- Track progress with visual progress bars
- Link daily priorities to bigger goals
- Share goals with family or keep them personal

**Habit Tracking That Sticks**

Build consistency with your non-negotiable habits. Track daily completions, watch your streaks grow, and celebrate milestones together. The gamification system makes building good habits fun for the whole family.

- Track up to 10 daily habits
- Visual streak counters with fire emoji
- Milestone celebrations at 7, 30, 90+ days
- Drag-to-reorder habit priority
- At-risk indicators to protect your streaks

**Family Coordination**

Stay in sync with your household. Entmoot lets families share plans, assign tasks, and celebrate wins together. See what everyone is working on and support each other's growth.

- Create or join a family group
- Assign tasks to family members
- View family calendars together
- Invite members with role-based access
- Switch between multiple families

**Reflection & Reviews**

Regular reflection drives growth. Capture quick thoughts anytime, complete structured evening reflections, and dive deep with weekly reviews. Track your wins, learn from challenges, and continuously improve.

- Quick reflection capture anytime
- Evening reflection with mood and gratitude
- Weekly review with metrics summary
- Track wins shipped and lessons learned
- Habit and goal completion analytics

**Gamification & Motivation**

Stay motivated with points, badges, and achievements. Earn rewards for completing goals, maintaining streaks, and consistent planning. Celebrate with confetti when you hit milestones!

- Earn points for every action
- Unlock badges for achievements
- Streak milestones with special celebrations
- Progress tracking and analytics
- Confetti celebrations for big wins

**Works Offline**

Plan your day even without internet. Entmoot syncs your changes when you're back online, so you're never blocked from getting organized.

**Privacy First**

Your family's data stays private. We use secure storage for sensitive information and never sell your data.

Download Entmoot today and transform how your family plans, grows, and achieves together!
