# Entmoot Landing Page PRD

## Vision

A whimsical, inviting landing page that captures the magic of family planning through the lens of "Lord of the Rings meets Minecraft" - where ancient tree wisdom meets blocky, buildable adventures. The Ents (tree shepherds) of Middle-earth were known for their patient, long-term thinking and community gatherings called "Entmoots." Combined with Minecraft's playful aesthetic of building block-by-block, we create a visual metaphor for families constructing their goals together, one step at a time.

---

## Design System

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Forest Green** | `#2D5A27` | Primary CTAs, headers |
| **Leaf Green** | `#7CB342` | Accents, highlights, success states |
| **Sky Blue** | `#64B5F6` | Secondary elements, links |
| **Warm Gold** | `#FFD54F` | Stars, achievements, special callouts |
| **Earth Brown** | `#795548` | Text, grounding elements |
| **Cream White** | `#FFF8E7` | Backgrounds, cards |
| **Sunset Orange** | `#FF7043` | Urgent CTAs, notifications |

### Typography

- **Headlines**: Bold, slightly rounded sans-serif (playful but readable)
- **Body**: Clean, friendly sans-serif
- **Accents**: Could incorporate subtle "blocky" pixel-style fonts for game-like elements

### Visual Elements

- **Blocky/Pixelated Trees**: Stylized trees that look like they could exist in Minecraft
- **Floating Islands**: Goal "levels" represented as floating landmasses
- **Treasure Chests**: For achievements/rewards
- **Pathways**: Stone brick paths connecting goals
- **Characters**: Simple, blocky family avatars (customizable silhouettes)
- **Day/Night Cycle**: Subtle gradient backgrounds suggesting morning planning â†’ evening reflection
